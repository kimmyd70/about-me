Welcome to the About Me Guessing Game

My name is Kim Damalas. My paternal grandfather immigrated from Thessaloniki in northern Greece and settled in Chicago. Our family is very proud of our Greek heritage. Personally, I'd rather I'd not inherited the Greek nose!

After graduating from college in 19-umm hmmm, I was commissioned an Officer in the Air Force and ultimately stuck it out for 25 years. It was a great career that allowed me to travel the world and work with some amazing minds in technical fields such:

  • Intercontinental Ballistic Missiles
  • Satellite Design and Operations
  • Space-Object Tracking Radars
  • National Missile Defense Systems
  • Cybersecurity

My last couple of positions were policy-oriented and very political, and I quickly decided it was time for a change. I retired in early 2017 and started looking for a second career that would allow me to work from home. For the last two years, my job has been "student"--first in Graphic Design and now in Software Development. I love learning! Not needing to pay for kids' college tuition, I'm able to use my military education benefits for myself.

I've been very fortunate to be formally educated in many areas--all paid for by the Air Force!

  • Bachelor's of Science in Math; minor in Computer Science (Iowa State University)
  • Master's of Science in Space System Design (University of North Dakota)
  • Master's of Science in Math (Naval Postgraduate School)
  • Master's of Science in Operations Research (Naval Postgraduate School)
  • Graphic Design (Seattle Central College)
  • Software Design (Code Fellows)
